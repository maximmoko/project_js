The Historic GOETZ THEATRE, on the National Historic Registry Lists, opened 02 September 1931. The facade to the main theatre building was dismantled then reconstructed October thru November 2006. Theatres 2 (renovated then re-opened 24 May 2007) and 3 were in place in October 1981 and December 1994 respectively, occupying previous buildings that all became part of The Goetz Building from 1931. This structure originally was a “mixed-use” project consisting of the Goetz Theatre, professional offices on the second floor with retail at street level.
